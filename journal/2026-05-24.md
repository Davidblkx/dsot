# Journal Entry - 2026-05-24

## Accomplishments
- **Database & Sync Transaction Refactoring**:
  - Designed and introduced the `DsotDatabaseTransaction` struct in `src/db_sync/src/database/transaction.rs` that encapsulates both `redb::WriteTransaction` and `sqlx::Transaction` to enable transactional atomicity across both Key-Value operations and SQL database updates.
  - Refactored the core logic of all CRUD and sync operations (like `insert`, `update`, `upsert`, `delete`, `restore`, `apply_journal`, `exec_op`) from `DsotDatabase` onto `DsotDatabaseTransaction` in `src/db_sync/src/database/entity_ops.rs`.
  - Implemented delegating wrappers in `DsotDatabase` that automatically instantiate a new `DsotDatabaseTransaction`, execute the targeted operation, and commit the transaction, ensuring 100% backward compatibility.
  
- **Batch Replication Support**:
  - Updated `ApplyJournalFn` signature in `src/db_sync/src/registry.rs` to accept a mutable borrow of `DsotDatabaseTransaction` instead of `&DsotDatabase`, enabling multiple operations to share the same open transaction.
  - Implemented `apply_journal_trx` and `apply_journals` in `RepositoryRegistry` to enable batching and replaying multiple serialized journal entries in a single atomic database transaction.
  - Updated the macro generator in `src/derive/src/sync_entity/generator.rs` to support the new transaction-based `APPLY_JOURNAL_REF` slice registration.
  - Fixed a stringification double-quoting bug in the procedural macro generated `APPLY_JOURNAL_REF` `table` field, resolving the registration of `"artists"` from `r#""artists""#` to `"artists"`.

- **Verification & Testing**:
  - Added `test_apply_journals_batch` to `src/model/src/entities/artist.rs` verifying successful batch replication of multiple operations in one atomic transaction.
  - Ran cargo checks and tests, confirming that everything compiles and passes cleanly with 0 errors and 0 warnings.
