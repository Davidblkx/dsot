## Architectural Shifts
- **Strict Separation of Sync Logic and Transport**:
  - Previously, `dsot_db_sync` contained Iroh endpoint wrappers and network streaming logic directly inside `sync/v1/iroh_sync_bridge.rs` and `iroh_protocol.rs`.
  - Now, `dsot_db_sync` is purely dedicated to database persistence, transaction journaling, and local state evaluation. It exposes an async trait `SyncNode` (`get_db_id` and `handle(&DBSyncMessage)`).
  - All networking, connection framing, and QUIC stream handling reside in `dsot_network`. `NetworkDBSyncNode` implements `SyncNode` over a framed `NetworkChannel`, allowing `SyncNodeHandler::sync` to reconcile local and remote databases without either layer knowing the implementation details of the other.
